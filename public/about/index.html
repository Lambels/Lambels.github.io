About

1 min · Patrick Arvatu

I’m Patrick Arvatu, a first-year Computer Science student at TU Delft with a focus on software engineering and sometimes trading.

Most of what I find interesting sits at the intersection of math, markets, and software. This means I find great interest in algorithms, concurrency and statistics. I love contributing to open source and building real life projects.

This blog is where I write up what I build and what I learn from building it. Posts tend to be technical and iterative: I start with something that works badly, show why it breaks, and fix it. The goal is to make the reasoning visible, not just the final code. Hopefully in this process I, together with the reader, gain insight in an interesting topic.

My main activity is on my github but feel free to reach me via LinkedIn or email.
